<Section icon={Target} title="The Wave 6 Reset Step-by-Step">

Executing the Wave 6 reset strategy correctly is vital for maximizing your pulls on the **anime paradox banner**. This method requires a specific setup to ensure you clear the early waves as fast as possible without over-investing in unit placements.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select the Map">
    Choose the first available map in Infinite mode. The layout is simple, and the enemy pathing is predictable, which allows for faster clear times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unit Placement">
    Place a strong early-game unit (like Broly or Megumin) near the enemy portal. The goal is to kill enemies the moment they spawn to shave seconds off the wave timer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Wave 6">
    Let the unit solo the waves until you hit Wave 6. You do not need to upgrade heavily; just ensure you have enough damage to prevent any leaks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Reset">
    Once Wave 6 is completed and you see the "+50 Gems" notification, immediately sell all units and let the enemies reach your base, or use the "Return to Lobby" button if it is faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Repeat">
    Re-enter the map and repeat the process. With practice, you can complete a full cycle in under 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
Using a unit with a large AOE (Area of Effect) at the spawn point is the most effective way to handle Wave 6 resets. This ensures that no individual enemy lingers on the map, keeping your clear time consistent.
</Callout>
