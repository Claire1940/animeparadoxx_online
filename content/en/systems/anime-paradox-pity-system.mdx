<Section icon={Swords} title="Banner Mechanics and Drop Rates">

While the pity system provides a safety net, understanding the base rates is still crucial for managing expectations. The **anime paradox pity system** works by incrementing a counter every time a non-Secret/Paradox unit is pulled. Once the threshold is reached, the next pull is guaranteed to be from the highest rarity tier available on that specific banner.

| Rarity Tier | Base Drop Rate | Pity Interaction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mythical** | Standard High % | Resets pity if pulled early |
| **Secret** | ~0.01% - 0.05% | Guaranteed at Pity Cap |
| **Paradox** | Extremely Low | Potential Pity Reward |
| **Portal Drop** | 1% (Combat Drop) | Not affected by Banner Pity |

<Section icon={Map} title="Ragnarok Shop and Farming Routes">

The Ragnarok Shop serves as a secondary progression system that complements the **anime paradox pity system**. Instead of relying on summons, you can farm specific drops from the Ragnarok mode to purchase units and items directly. This "grind-to-win" mechanic is highly favored by the community as it rewards active playtime.

| Shop Item | Cost Type | Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mythical Units** | Ragnarok Tokens | High (For team filling) |
| **Upgrade Materials** | Mode Drops | Essential (For unit leveling) |
| **Secret Portals** | Rare Drop (1%) | Very High (Access to Secret units) |
| **Special Potions** | Event Currency | Medium (Temporary boosts) |

The new map for the Ragnarok mode is significantly smaller than previous event maps. This compact design allows for faster wave clearing and more "kills per hour," which is vital since the Secret Portal drop rate is fixed at 1% per kill.

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
Focus on speed-clearing the Ragnarok map. Since the portal drop is per-kill, using high-AOE (Area of Effect) units will maximize your chances of obtaining a Secret Portal without touching the summon banners.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Summoning Optimization">

To make the most of the **anime paradox pity system**, you should follow a specific sequence of resource gathering and spending. Blindly rolling as soon as you have souls is inefficient and often leads to missing the pity cap.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Soul Accumulation">
    Farm at least 10 million to 20 million souls before attempting a serious run on Banner 2. This ensures you have enough to hit the pity threshold if your luck is poor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Banner Status">
    Verify that you are rolling on Banner 2. Look for the "Pity" text or counter on the UI. Do not roll on Banner 1 if your goal is a guaranteed Secret.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor the Counter">
    Keep track of your pulls. If you pull a Secret unit *before* hitting the pity cap, the counter will usually reset. This is the optimal time to stop and save souls for the next rotation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ragnarok Supplement">
    While your souls are low, switch to the Ragnarok mode. Farm the 1% portal drops to continue progressing your team without spending currency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Insight">

</Callout>
