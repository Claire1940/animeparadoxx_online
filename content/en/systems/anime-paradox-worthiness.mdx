Automating your **anime paradox worthiness** farming is the most efficient way to reach the end-game. By using a macro, you can loop the "Replay" function on defeat or victory, allowing your account to farm 24/7.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Software Preparation">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Recording the Loop">
    Start recording and place your core units. Begin with Bulma in a safe corner, then place your primary DPS (like Broly) at the start of the track. Upgrade them once and then stop recording.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Setting the Replay">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Testing and Optimization">
