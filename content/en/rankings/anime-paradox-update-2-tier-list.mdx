<Section icon={Target} title="Siege Mode Specialists: Qin Shi Huang & God Slayer">

Update 2 introduced a new Siege Mode where enemies can spawn with over 4 million health in later waves. To counter this, specific units have been designed to shred high-health targets. Qin Shi Huang and God Slayer are the MVPs of this mode.

**Qin Shi Huang** is unique because of his dual passives. He gains up to 40% increased damage when allies are nearby and deals a staggering 250% more damage to boss-type enemies. This makes him the fastest boss-killer in the game.

**God Slayer** remains "forever meta" due to his bleed mechanic. He applies a bleed effect that deals 5% of the target's health as damage. In a game where boss HP continues to scale, a percentage-based damage dealer will always be top-tier.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Support and Utility: Buddha's Role">

Buddha is the standout support unit of the Record of Ragnarok update. While he doesn't provide the raw numbers of Monkey King, his utility is indispensable for team-based play. He has a 25% chance to stop enemy attacks entirely and provides a 25% damage reduction buff to all allies for 6 seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Positioning Buddha">
    Place Buddha in a central location where his range covers as many allies as possible to ensure the damage reduction buff is applied consistently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trait Selection">
    Aim for the **Vampiric** or **Blessing** traits. Vampiric helps him stay on the field longer, while Blessing enhances his supportive capabilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Synergy Pairing">
    Pair Buddha with Hades. The 25% DR from Buddha stacks effectively with Hades' innate damage reduction, making your frontline nearly invincible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Unit Comparison">

</Callout>
