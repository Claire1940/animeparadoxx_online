### Stat Chip Priorities
Stat Chips (and Super Stat Chips) allow you to reroll individual grades (D through Z). You should aim for at least an **S-grade** in Damage and Range for your primary carry before moving on to your support units.
1. **Primary Carry**: Maximize Damage, Range, and Cooldown Reduction.
2. **Economy (Bulla)**: Focus solely on the Income stat.
3. **Supports**: Prioritize Cooldown Reduction and Range to ensure buffs and stuns hit the entire team.

<Section icon={Map} title="Evolution and Etherealization Steps">

Evolution in Anime Paradox X is a multi-stage process that requires specific materials from different game modes. Following the correct order ensures you don't waste resources on units you might replace later.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unit Leveling and Etherealize">
    Before a unit can be evolved, it must reach its level cap. Use the AFK Chamber or Infinite mode to farm experience quickly. Once capped, use the **Etherealize** menu to submit base materials like Essences and Gold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Targeted Material Farming">
    Identify the specific mode required for your unit's evolution item.
    - **JJK Units**: Farm Cursed Fingers in JJK Portals or the Sorcerer Shop.
    - **Sun Jin Wu**: Farm Raid Coins in Raid Act 3.
    - **Igrus**: Farm Siege Coins for Broken Helmets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Final Evolution Investment">
    Once evolved, the unit's stats and passives will drastically improve. This is the stage where you should begin using **Super Stat Chips** and **Mythic Trait Rerolls** to finalize the build.
  </Step>
</Steps>
