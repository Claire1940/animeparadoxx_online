<Section icon={Shield} title="Essential Support and Economy Rankings">

No **anime paradox tier list** is complete without acknowledging the units that keep your team alive and your wallet full. In Anime Paradox X, damage is only half the battle; without proper healing and income, even the strongest carries will eventually fail.

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Mistake">
Many new players fill all six slots with DPS units. This is a recipe for disaster in Raids and Infinite mode. Always reserve at least two slots for an economy unit (Bulla) and a healer or buffer (Idol/Oreheme).
</Callout>

| Role | Best Unit | Alternative | Why It's Necessary |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Economy** | **Bulla** | Sprintcart | Increases income to afford expensive upgrades. |
| **Healer** | **Oreheme** | Idol (Pop) | Keeps your units and base healthy during chip damage. |
| **Buffer** | **Idol (Pop)** | Gorhan | Increases attack speed and damage for the whole team. |
| **CC / Slow** | **Brojo** | Shadow Vampire | Stuns and slows dangerous enemies or bosses. |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Bulla Early">
    Bulla is a Legendary unit, making her relatively easy to obtain compared to Mythics. Level her up first in any match to maximize your Gold income.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Evolve Your Healer">
    A base Mythic healer is okay, but an evolved Oreheme or Idol provides significantly better sustain and secondary buffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Position for Buffs">
    Place your Idol or buffer in a central location where their range circle covers as many of your primary DPS units as possible.
  </Step>
</Steps>
