<Section icon={Map} title="Evolution Path for anime paradox meta units">

Unlocking the true potential of **anime paradox meta units** requires following a strict evolution and etherealization path. Most Secret units require specific materials found only in high-difficulty modes. For example, evolving **Megumo** into his Ten Shadows form requires 25 Heavenly Wheels, which can be a significant gold sink for new players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Leveling and Etherealization">
    Before evolving, level your unit to its current cap and submit the required Etherealize materials. This provides a permanent stat boost that carries over.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Material Farming">
    Target the specific mode for your unit. Farm **Hollow Legend Stages** for Ichuga, **Raid Act 3** for Sun Jin Wu, and **JJK Portals** for Sakuno.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Evolution Ritual">
    Once materials are gathered, use the Evolution menu. This will unlock the unit's final passive, such as Vegito's Transformation or Storrk's Resurreccion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Optimization">
    After evolution, use your saved **Trait Rerolls** and **Super Stat Chips** to lock in S-tier grades and the Eternal or Vampiric trait.
  </Step>
</Steps>
