<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solo Strategy">

Executing a solo run in the **anime paradox solo legend stage** requires precise timing, especially in the first few waves where your economy is vulnerable. Follow these steps to ensure your defense holds while you build your financial foundation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Placement">
    Immediately place **Bulma** before the countdown ends. You need every second of money generation to afford your first Broly placement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="First DPS Hook">
    Place one **Broly** near the core. Position him so his 180-degree arc covers the primary path where enemies cluster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Economy Scaling">
    Continue upgrading Bulma until she reaches **Level 3**. Do not spend money on other units until this milestone is achieved, as the income jump is significant.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dual Broly Setup">
    Place a second Broly on the opposite side of the core. Between the two, you should now have near-total coverage of the central area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Fodder Wall">
    Spread your low-cost fodder units around the perimeter. Their only job is to take hits and slow down enemies that might bypass your Brolys.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Timing">
