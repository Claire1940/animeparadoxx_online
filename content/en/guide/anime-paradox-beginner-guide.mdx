<Section icon={Target} title="Core Progression Steps">

Progression in Anime Paradox follows a linear path that transitions from story-based challenges to resource-intensive endgame modes. To move through this content effectively, you must understand the relationship between your account level and the modes you have unlocked.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Story Mode Grind">
    Focus entirely on clearing the Story mode from Act 1 to Act 6. This is the fastest way to gain account experience. Megumi can handle these stages solo if you place her strategically at corners or intersections.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking Codes and Traits">
    Upon hitting Level 5, redeem all available community codes. Use the resulting Trait Rerolls on your best current unit. If you are using Megumi, rolling for damage-boosting traits will significantly decrease your clear times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Accessing Weekly Challenges">
    Push your story progress until you unlock Planet Namek Act 3. This is the prerequisite for the Weekly Challenge stage. This stage is vital because it drops the specific resources required to evolve Megumi and other starter units into their more powerful forms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transitioning to Siege Mode">
    Once your units are evolved, enter the Ragnar Siege Mode. This mode is the primary source of endgame currency and portals. It is significantly harder than the story mode and requires a solid understanding of unit traits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Evolution Warning">
Never evolve a unit before checking its stats. Use the stat reroll system to ensure your unit has high base damage or range, as these stats carry over and become even more impactful after evolution.
</Callout>

</Section>

The most sought-after trait in Anime Paradox is **Ethereal**. This trait is a game-changer for Siege mode because it prevents enemies from attacking your base as long as the Ethereal unit is active on the field. Instead, enemies will redirect their attacks toward the Sukuna or Death Reaper unit, allowing you to sustain your base health indefinitely.

<Callout type="success" title="Trait Strategy">
The pity for the Ethereal trait is approximately 15,000 rerolls. While this seems high, you can farm hundreds of rerolls weekly through Siege mode and the portal system. Always prioritize rolling Ethereal on your highest DPS unit first.
</Callout>
