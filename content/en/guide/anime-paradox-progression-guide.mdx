<Section icon={Target} title="End-Game Strategy and Ethereal Trait Mastery">

Reaching the end-game requires a deep understanding of unit traits. The single most important trait for high-level progression is Ethereal. When placed on a meta unit like Sukuna or Death Reaper, the Ethereal trait alters enemy behavior completely.

Normally, enemies bypass your defenders and strike directly at your base. However, if a unit carries the Ethereal trait, all enemies on the map are drawn directly to that unit, ignoring the base entirely. This allows you to group enemies efficiently and eliminate them with AOE attacks, making solo farming viable even on the hardest difficulties.

To obtain Ethereal, you must engage with the trait reroll system. Reroll tokens can be purchased from the in-game shop. The shop offers a pack of 400 rerolls for a relatively low cost of basic currency, alongside a secondary pack of 200 rerolls. While the pity system guarantees a top-tier trait at 15,000 rolls, most players secure Ethereal much earlier with average luck.

<Callout type="warning" title="Placement Warning">
Do not attempt to solo high-level siege modes without at least one Ethereal unit placed near the front lines, as fast-moving enemies will easily slip past and destroy your base.
</Callout>
