<Section icon={Swords} title="Megumi & Maharaga Unit Breakdown">

Megumi Fushiguro stands out as one of the most versatile units introduced in this update. Operating under a strict one-placement limit, Megumi's primary strength lies in his dual-state combat design. He functions as a high-frequency ranged attacker before transitioning into a summoning platform for the Divergent Divine General Maharaga.

When Megumi attacks, he builds up damage stacks rapidly. Once he enters his Shadow State, his damage output and cooldown reduction receive massive boosts, making him an exceptional wave-clear unit.

| Unit Form | Attack Speed | Core Passive | Damage Multiplier |
| Megumi | Fast | Shadow State (+40% DMG, +20% CDR) | Up to **30x** stacks |
| Maharaga | Moderate | Critical Health Buff (+12% per 15% HP lost) | **+75%** bonus on 6th hit |

Once Maharaga is summoned via the Divine General ability, Megumi temporarily leaves the field, replaced by a tanking behemoth. Maharaga features unique scaling mechanics that make him progressively harder to defeat as the battle continues. For every percentage of health lost, Maharaga's damage reduction and raw power scale upward, making him the premier choice for high-wave infinite modes.
