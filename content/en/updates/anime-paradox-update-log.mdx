<Section icon={Target} title="Obtainment and Progression Guide">

Acquiring these new units requires a mix of Raid participation and luck in specific wards. The **anime paradox update log** specifies that while some units have a pity (PT) system, others are strictly RNG-based.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Raid Coins">
    Enter the **Raid Chamber** to farm Raid Coins. Both **Juzo** and **Amon** are available in the Raid Shop for 5,000 coins each.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Ward 13 Drops">
    Head to **Ward 13** for a chance at **Ken Kaneki** and **Eto**, both of whom have a 1% base drop rate. Note that Eto does not currently have a pity system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Evolution Items">
    Purchase evolution items like the **Kaneki Cuffs**, **Ghoul Eye**, and **Suitcase** from the Raid Shop to unlock the full potential of your units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Arima Pity System">
    Unlike Eto, **Arima** features an 80 PT (Pity) system in Ward 13, ensuring a guaranteed drop after a set number of runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Tip">
Always prioritize evolving **Eto** first. Her evolved form grants significant crowd control through **Owl's Shriek**, which stuns all enemies in range for 3 seconds when her health drops below 30%.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Character Balance Changes: Nerfs & Buffs">

This **anime paradox update log** brings some of the most significant balance shifts in the game's history. The most notable change is the massive nerf to **Sung Jin Woo**, whose shadow-based passives have been cut by nearly 50%.

### Major Nerfs
The development team has targeted high-performing secret units to bring them in line with the current meta.

| Character | Skill/Passive | Change Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sung Jin Woo** | Exalted Passive | Nerfed from 25% to 12.5% HP/DMG |
| **Sung Jin Woo** | Mutilation | Cooldown increased from 5s to 8s |
| **Sung Jin Woo** | Truth Cut | Cooldown increased from 20s to 24s |
| **Mahito** | Black Flash | Cooldown adjusted to 6s |
| **Yuta** | Damage Scaling | Nerfed from 3x to 2.5x multiplier |
| **Toji** | Pistol Damage | Nerfed from 3x to 2x damage scaling |

### Major Buffs
Conversely, several underperforming units have received substantial improvements to their damage output and cooldowns.

| Character | Skill/Passive | Change Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mahoraga** | Damage Buff | Drastically increased from 15% to 25% |
| **Sukuna** | New Upgrade | Added Upgrade 13: 12% DMG + 2% Range |
| **Stark** | HP/DMG Buff | Non-skill upgrades buffed from 8% to 15% |
| **Sagiri** | Execution | Cooldown reduced to 6s, 2.5% DMG buff |
| **Gabimaru** | Hollow Barrage | Cooldown halved from 12s to 6s |

<Callout type="warning" title="Meta Alert">
The **Sung Jin Woo** nerf is devastating for players who rely on shadow-stacking for Infinite mode. You may need to transition to a **Sukuna** or **Mahoraga** centered build to maintain high wave clears.
</Callout>
