<Section icon={Target} title="Portal Progression and Secret Units">

To unlock the most powerful characters like Sukuna and Gojo, you must master the JJK Portal system. There are three primary ways to acquire these portals, though some methods are significantly more efficient than others.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Acquisition">
    You can purchase basic JJK Portals from the Sorcerer Shop using currency obtained from codes. New players can get up to 140,000 currency instantly via active codes, which is enough to buy 10 portals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Climbing Tiers">
    Play through the portals to increase their tier. There is a 50% chance to receive a portal of the next tier upon completion. To reach the secret units, you must reach at least Tier 13.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlocking Secret Units">
    Once you have a Tier 15 portal, the drop rate for Itadori becomes 100%. For Gojo and Sukuna, you need to farm portals Tier 13 and above.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Community Strategy">
The most efficient way to reach Tier 15 is by using the official Discord's "Portal Finder" channel. Joining other players' high-tier portals saves hours of grinding through lower levels.
</Callout>

| Portal Tier | Difficulty | Notable Drops | Recommended Strategy |
