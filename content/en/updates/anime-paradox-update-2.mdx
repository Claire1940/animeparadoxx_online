<Section icon={Swords} title="New Secret Units: Stats and Abilities">

The core of the **anime paradox update 2** lies in its Secret units. Unlike standard pulls, these units often require evolution from Mythic bases or direct drops from difficult Portals. Each unit brings a unique flavor to the battlefield, ranging from pure damage to complex support-dps hybrids.

| Unit Name | Rarity | Placements | Primary Role | Key Mechanic |

### Poseidon: The Sea God
Poseidon stands out as one of the most consistent damage dealers in update 2. His massive range (graded S) allows him to cover significant portions of the map without needing many range-boosting traits. His passive, **God Above All**, rewards you for making him the strongest unit in his vicinity, granting a 25% damage increase.

### Guan Sha Wang: The First Emperor
Guan Sha Wang is the most complex unit introduced. He functions as a "battery" for your team, where his **Army of One** passive boosts the damage of all nearby allies by up to 32%. However, his **Emperor Sight** ability is a double-edged sword: it grants a massive 35% critical chance but drains 3% of his Max HP every 2 seconds.

<Callout type="warning" title="Unit Synergy Warning">
Because Guan Sha Wang drains his own health to maintain his crit buffs, he must be paired with a healer like Orihime or a unit with the Vampiric trait to ensure he doesn't self-destruct during long waves.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="The God Slayer Controversy: Soji Okita Analysis">

One of the most discussed additions in the **anime paradox update 2** is Soji Okita, the God Slayer. While his damage potential is top-tier, his **Ego** passive makes him a "selfish" unit that can actually nerf your other characters if not managed correctly.

| Passive Skill | Effect Description | Strategic Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **God Slayer** | +4% damage every 5s vs active bosses (Max 40%) | Excellent for late-game bosses |
| **Ego** | Reduces stronger allies' damage by 20% | Requires Soji to be your #1 DPS |
| **Red Tyrant** | 5% Max HP bleed over 5 seconds | Essential for high-health enemies |

Soji's **Ego** passive is a unique mechanic where any allied unit within his range that deals more damage than him has their own damage reduced by 20%. In exchange, Soji gains a 10% reduction in his Seconds Per Attack (SPA). This forces players to build their entire team around Soji as the primary carry. If you have a hyper-buffed Sukuna or another Secret unit, Soji might actually decrease your total team DPS.

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Verdict">
Soji Okita is a specialized tool. He is arguably the best boss killer in the game due to the 5% Max HP bleed, but he is a "no-go" for teams that rely on multiple spread-out damage dealers. Use him only if you can guarantee he remains the strongest unit on the field.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Obtainment Guide: Portals and Evolutions">

Getting the new units in **anime paradox update 2** requires navigating the new Portal system. These portals drop from standard maps and provide a guaranteed path to the Record of Ragnarok cast if you can clear the challenges.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Lobby Portal Farming">
    Farm standard maps to obtain the God Slayer Portal, Hades Portal, or China's Palace Portal. These are consumable items that open a lobby domain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Host the Domain">
    The Secret unit drops (Soji Okita, Guan Sha Wang) are primarily granted to the **Host** of the portal. Ensure you are the one using the item to maximize your drop rates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect Essences">
    New units require specific Essences for evolution. For example, Poseidon requires Sea God Essence to transition from his Mythic state to his Awakened Secret state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Awaken the Units">
    Use the evolution menu to combine your base unit with the required materials. Guan Sha Wang changes from a Rainbow rarity to Red (Secret) once awakened.
  </Step>
</Steps>
