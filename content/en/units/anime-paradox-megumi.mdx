<Section icon={Swords} title="Abilities and Passive Mechanics">

The core strength of **anime paradox megumi** comes from his passive ability, which allows for infinite scaling during a single wave. Each attack landed by Megumi grants a 3% damage increase. This effect stacks up to 30 times, meaning a fully ramped-up Megumi is dealing 90% more damage than his base stats suggest. However, players must maintain aggression, as these stacks reset after 6 seconds of inactivity.

Every 20 seconds, Megumi enters his signature **Shadow State** for a duration of 10 seconds. During this window, he gains:
- **40% Damage Increase**: This stacks multiplicatively with his basic passive.
- **20% Cooldown Reduction**: Allows for rapid-fire skill deployment.
- **Snake Bite Trigger**: Every fifth attack deals an additional 60% bonus damage and reduces the target's damage reduction by 20%.
