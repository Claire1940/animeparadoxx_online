<Section icon={Swords} title="Top Tier Unit Analysis: Gojo and Sukuna">

When discussing the top of the food chain in the 2026 meta, Gojo and Sukuna are the primary contenders. Gojo is not just a damage dealer; he is a utility powerhouse. His "Infinity" passive distorts space for 60 seconds, slowing enemies significantly as they approach. Furthermore, his immunity to all status effects (stun, slow, burn) ensures he remains active even in the most chaotic encounters.

Sukuna, on the other hand, focuses on overwhelming force and debilitating debuffs. His "Dismemberment" ability is particularly lethal, permanently reducing enemy damage by 25% while increasing the damage they receive by 25%. This makes him an essential pick for boss encounters where survivability is tied to how quickly you can weaken the target.
