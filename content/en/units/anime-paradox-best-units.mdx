<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step: Building Your Meta Team">

Building a team around the **anime paradox best units** requires a balanced approach. You cannot simply fill six slots with DPS and expect to clear late-game Portals or Siege. Follow this progression route to ensure your squad is ready for any challenge.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Your Economy">
    Equip **Bulla** in your first slot. Without her, you will struggle to afford the final upgrade tiers for S+ units like Storrk or Zid during a match. Aim for the **Greed** trait as soon as possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Your Main Carry">
    Choose one primary DPS that fits the mode. For Story, **Ciel (Super)** is excellent. For Infinite, you need **Sun Jin Wu** or **Super Vegito**. This unit should receive 80% of your Stat Chips and XP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Add Crowd Control and Support">
    Fill the next two slots with **Brojo** for stuns and **Idol** for damage buffs. These units act as force multipliers, making your main carry significantly more effective.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Slot in a Healer">
    For Raids and Siege, **Oreheme** is non-negotiable. Her ability to keep your frontline alive against boss AOE attacks is the difference between a win and a wipe.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Flex Slot">
    Use the final slot for mode-specific needs. In Siege, use **Zid** for wide AOE. In Boss Rush, use **Bumta** for single-target burst.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Upgrade Order">
Inside a match, always upgrade your **Economy unit** to its first two breakpoints before focusing on your DPS. This ensures you have the cash flow to react to sudden enemy threats in later waves.
</Callout>

</Section>
