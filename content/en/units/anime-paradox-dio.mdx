<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain and Evolve Dio">

Acquiring Dio requires a mix of luck in the Summon Zone and strategic resource management. Since he is a Legendary unit, the pity system will eventually guarantee him, but maximizing his potential requires evolution.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summoning Strategy">
    Use the `give gems, please` code to secure 7,000 starting gems. Spend these in the Summon Zone during banners that feature Legendary units. Enable "Auto-delete" for Rares and Commons to save inventory space.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stat Rerolling">
    Head to the Stat Reroll NPC. Use your accumulated gold to aim for at least A-rank in Range and Cooldown. Do not settle for C-rank stats if you plan on using Dio for Nightmare mode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Material Farming">
    Farm Story Mode stages to collect **Hollow Bones** and **Essence**. These are the primary materials required for the evolution of Legendary units into their "Super" or "Sacred" forms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evolution Crafting">
    Visit the Evolve area in the lobby. You will need approximately 25 specific materials (varying by unit) to complete the evolution. Unlike some other games, Anime Paradox evolution does not require a specific kill count.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Evolution Tip">
Evolved units retain their traits and stats. Always reroll your stats *before* evolving to ensure you don't waste rare materials on a low-stat base unit.
</Callout>

</Section>
