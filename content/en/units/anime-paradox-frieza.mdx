<Section icon={Swords} title="Abilities and Passive Mechanics">

The true strength of **anime paradox frieza** lies in his passive ability, **Explosive Aura**. This skill triggers every time Frieza attacks, dealing 30% of his total damage to every enemy within half of his total range. When paired with the Eternal trait's 300 range, this "half-range" is 150 units, which is larger than the total range of most other Mythic units.

Furthermore, this passive applies a **Burn effect**, dealing an additional 30% damage over 4 seconds. This ensures that even high-health bosses are constantly losing HP while they are within his massive influence zone.

**Active Abilities:**
1.  **Relentless Strikes**: A high-speed melee combo dealing approximately 40k damage at max level.
2.  **Deadly Disc**: A homing projectile that slices through multiple enemies for 50k damage.
3.  **Supernova**: A massive energy ball that nukes a targeted area for 40k+ damage with high knockback.

  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Base Unit">

  <Step num="2" title="The Eternal Reroll">

  <Step num="3" title="Triple Ascension">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="In-Game Upgrade Path">

  </Step>
