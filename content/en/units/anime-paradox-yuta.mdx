<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Passives">

The power of **anime paradox yuta** lies in his passives. Unlike Gojo, who relies on raw burst, Yuta is a scaling monster. The longer the battle lasts, the stronger he becomes. This makes him the premier choice for long-form content like Infinite Mode or Boss Raids.

**Key Passives Breakdown:**
- **Pure Love**: Increases energy/damage by 5% every 6 seconds. This stacks up to **40%**, providing a massive mid-game power spike.
- **Seven Strikes**: Every 7th attack deals 50% bonus damage and reduces the target's damage reduction by 25%.
- **Ultimate Love**: Upon reaching max stacks, Yuta gains 35% HP and 45% damage recession, making him nearly unkillable.

<Callout type="info" title="Combat Strategy">
Place Yuta early in your defense line. Because his "Pure Love" passive requires time to stack, he needs to be on the field as long as possible to reach that 40% damage bonus.
</Callout>
