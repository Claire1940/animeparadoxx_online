<Section icon={Swords} title="Anime Paradox Natsu Stats, Passives, and Mechanics">

Understanding the unique passives of **anime paradox natsu** is critical to maximizing his combat effectiveness. He functions as a primary lane-holder, excelling at melting large groups of enemies through a combination of damage-over-time (DoT) burns and explosive area-of-effect (AoE) triggers.

His kit is built around two core passives:
- **Burn Passive**: All active abilities apply a burn effect that inflicts 75% of his base damage over a 4-second duration. Furthermore, Natsu gains a 10% global damage increase for every burning enemy within his attack range, capping at a maximum boost of 50%.
- **Scorch Passive**: Every successful basic attack applies a stack of Scorch to the target. Upon applying the third stack, the target explodes, dealing 50% of Natsu's base damage to all surrounding enemies in a moderate radius.
