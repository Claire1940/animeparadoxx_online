<Section icon={Map} title="Acquisition and Evolution Path">

To obtain Geto, players must roll on the active Gem Banner during the Jujutsu Kaisen event rotation. He shares this banner with other themed units, such as Jogo and Hakari. Once you secure your base Geto unit, your primary objective should be upgrading him to his evolved state, Geto Parade.

Evolving Geto requires specific materials that can only be obtained through the Subway Portal system. These portals scale in difficulty from Tier 1 up to Tier 13 and beyond. Clearing higher-tier portals yields Curse currency, which is spent in the Event Shop to purchase Dragon Eyes—the essential evolution item for Geto.
