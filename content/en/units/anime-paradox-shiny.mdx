<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution Mechanics: The Moonlight Piano">

Evolution is the process of transforming a high-rarity unit into its ultimate form. For units like Cid Kagenou, this involves crafting complex items like the Moonlight Piano. <Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Base Essences">
    Collect Green, Red, and Purple Essences from daily challenges and the Essence shop. Green is the most common, while Red is required for high-tier crafting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Craft the Evolution Item">
    For Cid, you must craft the **Moonlight Piano**. This requires 8 Red Essences, 10 Purple Essences, and a variety of Rainbow and Yellow Essences.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Level Up the Unit">
    Ensure your base unit is at the required level. Evolution often resets the level or requires a minimum threshold to trigger the transformation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Evolution">
    Use the evolution menu to combine the unit with the crafted item. This will result in the "Shadow" or "Atomic" version of the character.
  </Step>
</Steps>
