In the **anime paradox death reaper** update, rolling the correct trait on your units is just as important as the units themselves. Traits can completely alter a character's playstyle, turning a mediocre damage dealer into an unstoppable force. The two standout traits in the current meta are Eternal and Vampiric.

  <Card title="Eternal Trait">
    - **Best for Pure DPS**: Ideal for units like Super Vegito, Storrk, and Zid.
    - **Key Benefit**: Provides massive stat scaling and cooldown reduction.
    - **Combat Style**: Maximizes raw damage output to clear waves quickly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vampiric Trait">
    - **Best for Frontline/Utility**: Ideal for Hades, Maeto, and Kasheemu.
    - **Key Benefit**: Grants life-steal and damage reduction based on hits.
    - **Combat Style**: Ensures high survivability in prolonged boss fights.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Greed Trait">
    - **Best for Economy**: Only equip this on dedicated farm units.
    - **Key Benefit**: Significantly increases gold and resource generation.
    - **Combat Style**: Not meant for combat; use purely for resource pooling.
  </Card>

Choosing between Eternal and Vampiric depends entirely on your unit's role within your team composition. Pure damage dealers who sit safely in the backline should always run Eternal. Frontline brawlers or units that need to take hits to ramp up their damage perform significantly better with Vampiric.

| Trait | Rating | Primary Effect | Best Suited Units |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Eternal** | ★★★★★ | Massive stat scaling & cooldown reduction | Super Vegito, Storrk, Zid, Kisoge |
| **Vampiric** | ★★★★☆ | Life-steal & damage reduction | Hades, Maeto, Kasheemu, Itochi |
| **Greed** | ★★★☆☆ | Increased gold and resource generation | Dedicated farm units only |
