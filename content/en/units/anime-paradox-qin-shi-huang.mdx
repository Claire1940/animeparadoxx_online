<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Siege Mode">
    Head to the Ragnarok matchmaking area and start a Siege Mode run. This mode features infinite waves where enemies scale in health and speed over time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Takedowns">
    Portals have a 0.1% drop rate per kill. To guarantee a drop, you should aim for at least 1,000 to 3,000 takedowns in a single run. The more kills you secure, the higher your chance of seeing a portal in your inventory upon leaving.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate China King's Portal">
    Check your inventory for the "China King's Portal." This is a specialized 30-wave challenge. Ensure you bring a farm unit like Bulma, as the entry cost for units within the portal is high.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Portal">
    Finish all 30 waves of the China King's Portal. Upon completion, the base version of Qin Shi Huang will be added to your inventory.
  </Step>
</Steps>
