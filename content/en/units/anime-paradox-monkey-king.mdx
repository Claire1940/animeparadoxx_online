<Section icon={Shield} title="Sun Wukong Passives & Survival Mechanics">

What sets the Monkey King apart from other high-damage units is his dual-passive system. These passives focus on defensive evasion and self-sustain, making him an excellent frontline defender in high-tier portals.

His first passive is a dodge and counter mechanic. Every 12 seconds, Wukong will automatically dodge the next incoming attack and counter with a random ability. However, this skill carries a risk: there is a 20% chance the dodge will fail, resulting in Wukong being stunned for 2 seconds.

His second passive, Elixir of Life, provides incredible sustain. Wukong heals for 5% of his maximum HP every time he secures 10 kills. This makes him highly self-sufficient compared to units like Sung Jinwoo, whose life steal only activates when their health drops to critically low levels.

| Unit Name | Healing Trigger | Health Restored | Drawbacks |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| Monkey King | Every 10 kills secured | 5% of Max HP | Requires final blow on enemies |
| Sung Jinwoo | Low HP threshold only | Variable life steal | Only triggers at near-death states |

<Callout type="info" title="Sustain Comparison">
Wukong does not need to drop to low health to begin healing. As long as he is actively defeating enemies, his health pool remains topped off, making him safer against burst damage.
</Callout>

</Section>
