<Section icon={Target} title="Eto Evolution Requirements and Materials">

Unlocking the base version of Eto is only the first step. To unlock her true potential and access her monstrous Kakuja form, you must evolve her. The evolution process significantly boosts her base damage, range, and attack speed, transforming her from a niche utility unit into a top-tier damage dealer.

The primary evolution material required for Eto is the **Ghoul Eye**. This exclusive item cannot be obtained through standard stages; instead, you must purchase it directly from the Raid Shop using currency earned by completing Tokyo Ghoul Raid acts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Base Eto">
    Farm Act 2 of the Tokyo Ghoul Raid until the base Eto unit drops into your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Raid Currency">
    Complete raid acts repeatedly to accumulate at least **750 Raid Currency**. Both Act 1 and Act 2 reward this currency upon successful completion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase the Ghoul Eye">
    Navigate to the Raid Shop in the main lobby and purchase the Ghoul Eye evolution material for 750 Raid Currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Gather Standard Essences">
    Ensure you have the required standard essences in your inventory. These can be obtained through daily quests, challenges, or standard map drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evolve via the NPC">
    Interact with the Evolution NPC in the lobby, select Eto from your unit list, and confirm the evolution to unlock her Kakuja form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Eto Abilities, Passives, and Combat Performance">

Eto stands out from other Secret units due to her dynamic battle mechanics. Instead of offering static stat lines, her performance shifts fluidly based on her current health status. This high-risk, high-reward playstyle makes her incredibly versatile in both standard defense maps and high-stress raid scenarios.

Her kit is designed to reward players who can manage unit positioning and handle waves that occasionally breach outer defenses. Additionally, her built-in debuffing capabilities make her an essential asset for taking down high-HP bosses.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="High Health State (70%+) ">
    When Eto remains above 70% HP, she gains a **20% Range Buff**. This allows her to strike enemies from a distance and cover multiple lanes simultaneously.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mid Health State (Under 70%)">
    If her health drops below 70%, she shifts focus, gaining a **15% Cooldown Reduction**. This significantly increases her attack frequency and overall DPS.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Critical State (Under 30%)">
    When dropping below 30% HP, she unleashes a scream that **stuns all enemies** in her range for 3 seconds. This serves as an excellent emergency defense.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Beyond her health-based scaling, Eto possesses a powerful marking passive. Every five basic attacks, she marks the strongest enemy within her range. This mark forces the target to take **15% increased damage** from all sources for 6 seconds. This utility makes her a premier choice for boss-killing setups.

| Passive Name | Trigger Condition | Effect Granted | Duration / Cooldown |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Apex Predator** | HP above 70% | **+20% Range** | Permanent while above 70% HP |
| **Adrenaline Rush** | HP below 70% | **+15% Cooldown Reduction** | Permanent while below 70% HP |
| **Kakuja Scream** | HP drops below 30% | **3-Second AoE Stun** | Emergency trigger (once per wave) |
| **Owl's Mark** | Every 5 basic attacks | **+15% Damage Taken by Target** | 6-Second Duration |

<Callout type="info" title="Synergy Breakdown">
Eto's marking passive synergizes perfectly with high-DPS units like Sukuna or Awakened Kaneki. By amplifying the damage bosses take, she allows your primary damage dealers to shred through massive health pools in seconds.
</Callout>
