<Section icon={Shield} title="Abilities, Passives, and Combat Meta">

What makes **anime paradox gojo** a top-tier unit in 2026 is his utility. While some units offer higher raw DPS, Gojo provides crowd control and survivability that is unmatched. He is a "one-placement" unit, meaning you can only have one of him on the field, but his impact covers the entire map.

**Key Abilities:**
- **Infinity (Passive)**: Distorts space around Gojo. Enemies within his range have their movement speed increasingly slowed the closer they get to him.
- **Infinite Void (Domain)**: A massive active ability that stuns all enemies within range for 60 seconds. It also deals 1,000% burst damage upon activation.
- **Master of the Limitless**: Grants Gojo a 25% damage buff and 15% cooldown reduction. Every 20 seconds, his next ability deals 40% increased damage.
