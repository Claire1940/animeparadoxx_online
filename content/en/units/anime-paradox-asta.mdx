<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Tactics: Shield Stripping Mastery">

The core mechanic that makes **anime paradox asta** a permanent fixture in elite teams is his mastery over enemy defenses. In high-tier raids, enemies often spawn with layers of protection that mitigate up to 80% of incoming damage. Asta ignores these mechanics and removes them entirely for the rest of your team.

**Strategic Placement Tips:**
- **Early Path Placement**: Place at least one Asta near the enemy spawn portals to strip shields as early as possible.
- **Mid-Map Support**: Use the remaining two placements in the middle of the map to ensure any enemies that regenerated shields are stripped again.
- **Boss Focus**: During boss waves, concentrate Asta's fire on the boss to remove debuffs that might stun your other units.
