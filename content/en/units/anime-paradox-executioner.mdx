<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution Guide: Ken Kaneki to Eye Patch King">

To maximize the potential of your **anime paradox executioner**, you must evolve your units. For Kaneki, this involves a specific grind within the Tokyo Ghoul raid acts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base Kaneki">
    Farm Act 1 of the Tokyo Ghoul raid. While the drop rate is low, persistent grinding (estimated 6-7 hours) will eventually net you the base unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Raid Coins">
    You need **750 Raid Coins** to purchase the necessary evolution item. Currently, a bug in Act 3 allows for faster coin farming by completing 15 waves instead of 30.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase Kaneki Cuffs">
    Head to the Raid Shop and exchange your 750 coins for the **Kaneki Cuffs**. This is the unique evolution material required for his transformation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Evolution">
    Go to the Evolution menu, select Kaneki (Kan Beki), and spend **25,000 Gold** along with the Cuffs to transform him into **Kan Jaka (Eye Patch King)**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Evolution Stage | Name | Rarity | Key Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Base | Kan Beki | Mythic | Standard single-target attacks |
| Evolved | Kan Jaka | Secret | Unlocks "Ghoul Slam" and passive synergy |
| Max Level | Eye Patch King | Secret | Optimized stats (Level 50+) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Requirement">
After evolving, you may need to rejoin a new server for the unit's rarity to update correctly from Mythic to Secret in your inventory.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Traits and Stat Rerolls">

For an **anime paradox executioner**, your trait choice defines your efficiency. While many players chase the 0.1% Eternal trait, others find more value in sustain-based traits like Vampiric, especially for frontline units.

**Trait Priority List:**
1. **Eternal (0.1%)**: The gold standard. Provides the highest boost to damage, range, and cooldown reduction.
2. **Vampiric**: Essential for Maeto and Tuji to keep them alive while they stay in the thick of combat.
3. **Angelic**: A solid alternative for cooldown reduction if Eternal is out of reach.
4. **Godly**: Great raw damage boost, though lacking the utility of higher-tier traits.

| Trait Name | DMG Boost | Range Boost | CDR Boost | Special Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Vampiric** | ★★★★☆ | ★★★☆☆ | ★★★★☆ | Lifesteal/Healing on hit |
