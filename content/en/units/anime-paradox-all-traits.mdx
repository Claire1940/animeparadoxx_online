In Anime Paradox, traits function as passive multipliers that can completely transform a mediocre unit into an absolute powerhouse. These passive modifiers scale directly with your unit's base statistics, meaning a percentage-based boost becomes exponentially more valuable as your units level up. To maximize your team's efficiency in both story mode and infinite challenges, understanding how to acquire and optimize these traits is absolutely essential.

To begin modifying your units, you must locate the Trait Reroll NPC, **Oreheme**. She is positioned prominently in the main lobby, standing directly across the Level Milestones section and just to the left of the Play area. Interacting with Oreheme opens the trait customization interface, where you can select any unit in your inventory and roll for a new passive trait.

Every single reroll attempt consumes a Trait Reroll token. While these tokens can occasionally be earned by completing difficult in-game achievements or reaching specific level milestones, the most reliable and efficient method to accumulate them is by redeeming active promotional codes.

Optimizing your unit traits requires a systematic approach. Follow this step-by-step guide to efficiently roll for the best statistics without wasting your hard-earned resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Reroll Tokens">
    Before visiting the NPC, ensure you have redeemed all active codes and claimed your level milestone rewards. Aim to have at least 50 Trait Reroll tokens in your inventory to ensure a successful rolling session.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Oreheme in the Lobby">
    Spawn into the main lobby and head towards the left side of the Play area. Look directly across from the Level Milestones section to find Oreheme standing near the trait pedestal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Your Target Unit">
    Interact with Oreheme to open the trait interface. Carefully select the unit you wish to upgrade. Double-check that you have chosen the correct unit to avoid accidentally overwriting a valuable trait on another character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Rerolls and Evaluate">
    Begin rolling. If you are low on tokens, stop immediately if you hit a solid utility trait like **Attack III** or **Slayer**. If you have an abundance of tokens, continue rolling until you secure an S-tier exotic trait like **Vampiric** or **Eternal**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Rolling Strategy">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Specialized Trait Strategies for Farming & Support">

In Anime Paradox, achieving victory in late-game challenges requires more than just raw damage. You must build a cohesive team where every unit's trait complements its specific role. Having the wrong trait on a utility unit can severely cripple your economy or defensive capabilities.

For instance, putting a pure damage-boosting trait like **Attack III** on a gold-generating farm unit is a complete waste of resources. Instead, these units should exclusively run traits that boost utility, income, or team-wide defense.

| Trait Name | Designated Role | Key Team Benefit | Priority Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Greed** | Economy / Farm | Boosts wave income by **20%**, allowing faster max upgrades | **Essential (5/5)** |
| **Angelic** | Buffers / Supports | Increases all applied ally buffs by **25%** | **Excellent (5/5)** |
| **Blessing** | Healers / Sustain | Enhances global team healing by **30%** | **Good (3/5)** |

By matching your units with their optimal role-specific traits, you create a powerful synergy. Your farm units will generate cash rapidly, allowing you to quickly place and fully upgrade your S-tier DPS units carrying **Eternal** or **Vampiric** traits.
