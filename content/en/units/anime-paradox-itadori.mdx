<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolving Itadori to Sukuna">

Once you have successfully cleared Secret Portal 2 and claimed your Itadori unit, the next step is evolving him into his ultimate form: Sukuna, the Cursed King. Evolving this unit significantly boosts his baseline combat statistics and unlocks powerful passive traits that make him one of the strongest damage dealers in the game. 

To initiate the evolution, you must collect specialized evolution materials known as Fire Fingers (also referred to as Cursed Fingers). These items cannot be farmed directly from portal drops; instead, they must be purchased from the Sorcerer Shop using Cursed Energy. Cursed Energy is rewarded generously for clearing high-tier JJK portals and redeeming active promotional codes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Farm Cursed Energy">
    Grind Tier 8 or Tier 9 portals repeatedly to accumulate event currency. Alternatively, redeem active promo codes like the "sorry bundle" to instantly secure 40,000 Cursed Energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit the Sorcerer Shop">
    Navigate to the Sorcerer Shop in the main lobby. Purchase exactly 4 Fire Fingers from the vendor. Each finger costs 20,000 Cursed Energy, requiring a total investment of 80,000 Cursed Energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Evolution">
    Open your unit inventory, select Yuji Itadori, and open the evolution interface. Ensure all 4 Fire Fingers are in your inventory, then confirm the evolution to transform him into Sukuna.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Because Sukuna is limited to a single placement on the battlefield, certain traits perform significantly better than others. While utility traits like Vampiric offer excellent lifesteal, they ultimately limit his raw damage output in late-game infinite runs.

The absolute best-in-slot trait for Sukuna is the 0.1% Eternal trait. Eternal provides massive, unparalleled multipliers to both damage and health, transforming Sukuna into a self-sustaining powerhouse. If you are short on reroll tokens, Vampiric is a highly viable secondary option, but serious players should always save their tokens to aim for Eternal.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Sukuna Showcase: Moves & Passives">

Sukuna boasts some of the most sophisticated mechanics in the current update. His kit is built around high-impact AoE attacks, damage multipliers, and self-healing. Unlike standard DPS units that rely strictly on raw base damage, Sukuna utilizes a unique resource meter called Energy Flow to dynamically boost his combat efficiency.

His primary passive, Malevolent Shrine, applies a devastating bleed effect to all targets within his massive range, dealing 30% of his base damage over a 3-second duration. Additionally, he has a 5% chance on every hit to trigger a Black Flash. This ability not only deals explosive damage but also instantly restores 33% of his maximum health and completely fills his Energy Flow meter.
