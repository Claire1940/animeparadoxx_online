<Section icon={Swords} title="Support Mechanics and Meta Synergies">

The true strength of the **anime paradox orihime evo** lies in her passives. These abilities transform her from a simple healer into a mandatory offensive buffer. Understanding how to trigger these effects is the difference between a good player and a great one.

**Passive Abilities Breakdown:**
- **Shan Shanun Ra Shun**: Every 10 seconds, Orihime restores 10% health to allies and gains a 10% cooldown rate reduction for 6 seconds. Furthermore, every third heal grants a 3-hit shield that lasts for 10 seconds.
- **Sacred Support**: Every three attacks, she restores 10% health to all allies in range. If an ally is already at full health (overhealing), they receive a +30% damage buff for 6 seconds.

| Synergy Unit | Benefit Level | Reason for Synergy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Broly** | Excellent | High natural HP; benefits greatly from the 30% damage overheal buff. |
| **Ichigo** | Excellent | Constant self-sustain combined with Orihime's buffs creates an unkillable DPS. |
| **Tank Units** | Good | The 3-hit shield from Shan Shanun Ra Shun provides massive effective HP. |
| **Glass Cannons** | Moderate | Helps with survivability but they often lack the HP to benefit from %-based heals. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Overheal Strategy">
To maximize the 30% damage buff, place Orihime near units that have high health or self-healing mechanics. Since they are often at 100% HP, Orihime's heals will trigger the "Sacred Support" damage boost almost every time the passive activates.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Gameplay Strategies for Infinite Mode">

In Infinite Mode, the **anime paradox orihime evo** is essential for surviving the scaling damage of later waves. As enemies begin to one-shot standard units, the 3-hit shield and constant percentage-based healing become the only way to maintain a front line.

**Tactical Placement Tips:**
- **Central Positioning**: Place Orihime where her 71-range circle covers the maximum number of high-value DPS units.
- **Buffer Layering**: Use her in conjunction with other buffers. Her 30% damage boost usually stacks multiplicatively or additively with other sources, depending on the specific unit's kit.
- **Shield Management**: Monitor the 10-second internal timer for her shield. In waves with high attack speed enemies, the 3-hit shield might deplete instantly, so secondary CC (Crowd Control) is still required.
