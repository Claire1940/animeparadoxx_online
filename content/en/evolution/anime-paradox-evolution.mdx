<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Evolve Units: Step-by-Step">

The evolution process is handled through the Crafting and Evolution NPCs found in the main lobby. It is a two-part process: first, you must craft the specific evolution item, and second, you must apply that item to a max-level or ascended unit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Base Mythical">
    Summon on the rotating banners. For the best results, use Luck Potions and wait for banners featuring Zoldic or Okiora. If you have VIP, take advantage of the 20% summon discount.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Legendary Stages">
    Specific evolution materials like Broly Pelts only drop from Legendary Stages (e.g., Planet Namek Act 1). These stages are harder than standard story acts and require a well-leveled team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Colored Essences">
    Essences (Red, Blue, Green, Pink) are obtained through Daily and Weekly Challenges. You can also purchase limited quantities from the Gold Merchant, which resets every hour.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Craft the Evolution Item">
    Visit the Crafting NPC. Combine your gathered essences and boss drops to create the unique item (e.g., the Godspeed Yo-yo).
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Execute the Evolution">
    Go to the Evolution NPC, select your unit, and consume the item. Your unit will now feature a new model and enhanced abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>
